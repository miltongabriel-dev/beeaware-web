Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

BeeAware (“the App”) is designed with privacy by default. We do not require user accounts, names, email addresses, or any form of direct personal identification to use the App.

Information We Collect

BeeAware collects only the minimum information necessary to operate as a community safety awareness tool.

  • Community reports: Safety-related information submitted voluntarily by users.
  • Approximate location data: Location associated with reported incidents, limited to what is necessary to display incidents on the map.
  • Anonymous technical metadata: Limited technical information (such as timestamps or anonymized identifiers) used to prevent abuse, ensure system integrity, and support moderation.

How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Display safety-related incidents on the map;
  • Improve situational awareness for users;
  • Prevent misuse, spam, or abusive behavior;
  • Maintain the reliability and security of the service.

Data Sharing

BeeAware does not sell personal data and does not share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Community reports may become visible to other users after moderation delays or automated checks. Publicly available official data sources may also be displayed alongside community reports.

Data Retention

Community reports and related data may be stored for a limited period to support safety awareness, moderation, and system integrity. Data may be removed, aggregated, or anonymized over time.

Emergency Disclaimer

BeeAware is not an emergency service. The App does not monitor incidents in real time. Always contact local emergency services if you are in immediate danger.

Your Rights

As BeeAware does not collect direct personal identifiers, most data cannot be linked back to an individual user. Where applicable under data protection laws, you may contact us with questions or concerns regarding privacy.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
